My favorite sites in Washington DC

My GHY3814 Fall 2020 Final project - Douglas Hall

I was commissioned by a local newspaper to create an interactive map based on a recent story in the Washington Post about the dedication of the "Native American Veterans Memorial" on November 11, 2020.
The proposed map was to show the statues and sculptures of the Smithsonian Institution. As I researched this project, I realized there are hundreds of statues and sculptures. I convinced the editor to allow
me to map 13 of my favorite sites as multiple 'tourists' webmaps already exist.

I grew up in the Washington DC area. Needless to say school field trips included the Smithsonian Institution and all the monuments and memorials on the National Mall and elsewhere in the metro area.
I am a service veteran and an engineer. My 'favorites' may be a bit obscure or eclectic. I hope you enjoy this tour.
